Half-Life + Halo

A Message to the Community

This mod was a passion project begun and ended as a hobby. It spawned a wonderful community of contributors who wanted to see the mod succeed. After we both left the project, we believed the mod was in good hands at The Phoenix Project Software who is a group with a long line of experience in HL1 and GoldSrc Modding. Therefore, while we left with Beta 2's release, we encourage you to check out the work that Phoenix Project are doing going forward.

We are not a part of Phoenix Project nor endorse any fork of this mod! We are no longer involved with this project. Do not message us asking us for files, or to be involved in future efforts, or anything related to this mod.

If you are interested in playing our version, download Beta 2 of HaloGS. If you want to try out all of the work they've been doing at Phoenix Project, you can find them and their updated version here: website and github.

Thank you for all those who played, contributed, and made the discord so welcoming.

- Seal & GhostGerm
